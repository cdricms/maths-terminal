Exemple 9 - Type BAC

Limite de référence:

\(\lim\limits_{x \to +\infty} e^x = +\infty\)

  1. Prouver que: \(\lim\limits_{x \to +\infty} e^x = +\infty\).

\[ \begin{array}{l} e \approx 2,718 \\ \lim\limits_{n \to +\infty} e^n = +\infty \\ \text{ou encore:}\\ \lim\limits_{x \to +\infty} e^x = +\infty \end{array} \]

  1. Prouver que \(\lim\limits_{x \to -\infty} e^x = 0\)

On va poser \(x=-u\)

\[ \lim\limits_{x \to -\infty} e^x = \lim\limits_{u \to +\infty} e^{-u} = \lim\limits_{u \to +\infty} \frac{1}{e^u} = 0 \]

Limite de référence:

\(\lim\limits_{x \to +\infty} e^x = +\infty\)

  1. \(f(x) = 2e^x - \frac{1}{x}\) sur \([0; +\infty[\)

Limite en \(0\) et en \(+\infty\) ?

En \(0⁺\):

\[ \begin{rcases} \lim\limits_{x \to 0⁺ } 2e^x = 2 \\ \lim\limits_{x \to 0⁺ } \frac{1}{x} = +\infty \\ \end{rcases} \text{par différence: } \lim\limits_{x \to 0⁺} f(x) = -\infty \]

En \(+\infty\):

\[ \begin{rcases} \lim\limits_{x \to +\infty } 2e^x = +\infty \\ \lim\limits_{x \to +\infty } \frac{1}{x} = 0⁺ \\ \end{rcases} \text{par différence: } \lim\limits_{x \to +\infty} f(x) = +\infty \]
